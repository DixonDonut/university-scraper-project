# University Programme Database — Master Ruleset
*All rules applied consistently across every university in this project.*

---

## 1. INCLUDE — What to List

Include a programme if it meets **all** of the following:

- ✅ **Bachelor's degree** (BSc, BA, BBA, BCA, BFA, B.Com, B.Tech, LLB, or equivalent)
- ✅ **Associate Degree** (AA, AS, AAS, AAA) that opens directly to HS graduates
- ✅ **Diploma / Advanced Diploma** open to international HS graduates
- ✅ **D3** (Indonesia — 3-year diploma) open to international HS graduates
- ✅ **D4** (Indonesia — 4-year applied bachelor diploma) open to international HS graduates
- ✅ **Foundation Degree** (UK — FdA, FdSc) open to international HS graduates
- ✅ **Higher National Diploma (HND)** (UK) open to international HS graduates
- ✅ **Tandai / Junior College Degree** (Japan — 短期大学士, 2 years) open to international HS graduates
- ✅ **2-year College Diploma** (Korea — 전문학사) open to international HS graduates
- ✅ **Open to international high school graduates** (no prior tertiary qualification required)
- ✅ **Full-time** (part-time only = EXCLUDE)
- ✅ **On-campus** (distance/online only = EXCLUDE)
- ✅ **Minimum 8 months** duration
- ✅ **Combination/dual-subject degrees** open to HS graduates — INCLUDE
- ✅ **Dual degree programmes** (two degrees simultaneously) — INCLUDE
- ✅ **Teaching qualification programmes** that award a Bachelor of Education — INCLUDE
- ✅ **Evening programmes** open to HS graduates — INCLUDE
- ✅ **5- or 6-year dual degree programmes** open to HS graduates (e.g. Law + Economics integrated programmes in Europe) — INCLUDE

---

## 2. EXCLUDE — What to Omit

Exclude a programme if **any** of the following applies:

- ❌ **Masters / Postgraduate** (MA, MSc, MBA, MPhil, etc.)
- ❌ **PhD / Doctorate** (all doctoral programmes)
- ❌ **Staatsexamen** (Germany — state examination degree, not a standard bachelor)
- ❌ **Diplom** (old German degree — no longer offered but still appears on some sites)
- ❌ **Kirchliches Examen / Ecclesiastical-only seminary degrees**
- ❌ **Post-bachelor Diploma** (professional diplomas requiring a completed degree first)
- ❌ **Diploma top-up programmes** (require prior D3 or equivalent — not open to fresh HS grads)
- ❌ **Short certificates under 8 months** duration
- ❌ **Higher National Certificate (HNC)** (UK — too short, below diploma level)
- ❌ **Part-time only** programmes
- ❌ **Distance/online only** (PJJ in Indonesia, fully online programmes)
- ❌ **Top-up / Conversion programmes** (require prior tertiary qualification — D3 holders, etc.)
- ❌ **Requires prior tertiary degree** (e.g. 4-Year MD at TAU requiring 90+ undergraduate credits; RN-BSN nursing top-ups)
- ❌ **Restricted to citizens / residents** of a specific country
- ❌ **Taught entirely at a partner institution from Day 1**
- ❌ **BAES, BASUS** (Alliance Una Europa joint degrees — excluded by policy)
- ❌ **Employee Class / Kelas Karyawan** (Indonesia — working professional track)
- ❌ **Profession Study Program** (Indonesia — post-bachelor professional, e.g. NERS, Apoteker)
- ❌ **Conversion Class** (Indonesia — top-up for D3 diploma holders)
- ❌ **DEC-BAC** (Canada — requires prior DEC college diploma from Quebec)
- ❌ **Post-graduate certificates / diplomas** (Canada/Australia — require prior degree)
- ❌ **TAFE certificates under 8 months** (Australia)
- ❌ **6-year integrated Medicine / Dentistry / Veterinary** — EXCLUDE
  - Spain: 6yr Medicina, 5yr Odontología, 5yr Farmacia → EXCLUDE
  - Note: Short Premed + MD pathways open to HS grads (e.g. TAU 5yr, 5.5yr) = INCLUDE with NMC: Yes
- ❌ **Nivelación** (Spanish top-up nursing programme)
- ❌ **International Joint Degree where student is based at partner institution** from start
- ❌ **"Undergraduate Major in X"** listings that are minors or concentrations only

## 3. Course Name Formatting

### General Format
Use **Proper Title Case** throughout. Translate all non-English names to English.

### Degree Type Prefix Rules

**Approved abbreviations** (only when the university explicitly uses them):

| Abbreviation | Full Form | Condition |
|---|---|---|
| `BSc` | Bachelor of Science | University explicitly uses "BSc" or "B.Sc." |
| `BA` | Bachelor of Arts | University explicitly uses "BA" or "B.A." |
| `BBA` | Bachelor of Business Administration | University explicitly uses "BBA" |
| `BCA` | Bachelor of Computer Applications | University explicitly uses "BCA" |
| `BFA` | Bachelor of Fine Arts | University explicitly uses "BFA" |
| `AA` | Associate of Arts | University explicitly uses "AA" |
| `AS` | Associate of Science | University explicitly uses "AS" |
| `AAS` | Associate of Applied Science | University explicitly uses "AAS" |
| `AAA` | Associate of Applied Arts | University explicitly uses "AAA" |
| `FdA` | Foundation Degree in Arts | University explicitly uses "FdA" |
| `FdSc` | Foundation Degree in Science | University explicitly uses "FdSc" |
| `HND` | Higher National Diploma | University explicitly uses "HND" |

**Non-approved — always use full name:**
- `B.Com` → **"Bachelor of Commerce"**
- `B.Tech` → **"Bachelor of Technology"**
- `LLB` → **"Bachelor of Laws"** (unless university uses LLB on their pages)
- `D3` → **"Diploma III in [Subject]"**
- `D4` → **"Diploma IV in [Subject]"**

### Diploma and Associate Degree Formatting

| Degree Type | Format Example |
|---|---|
| Diploma | `Diploma in Hospitality Management` |
| Advanced Diploma | `Advanced Diploma in Business Administration` |
| Diploma III (Indonesia D3) | `Diploma III in Accounting` |
| Diploma IV (Indonesia D4) | `Diploma IV in Information Technology` |
| Associate of Arts | `AA in Psychology` |
| Associate of Science | `AS in Computer Science` |
| Associate of Applied Science | `AAS in Culinary Arts` |
| Foundation Degree Arts (UK) | `FdA in Early Childhood Studies` |
| Foundation Degree Science (UK) | `FdSc in Engineering` |
| HND (UK) | `HND in Business` |
| Junior College (Japan) | `Associate Degree in [Subject]` |
| 2-year College (Korea) | `Associate Degree in [Subject]` |

### If No Subject/Specialisation Listed
Use the **full degree name** without "in X":
- ✅ `Diploma in Business`
- ✅ `Associate of Science`

### If Subject Present
Use **abbreviation + "in" + subject** (or full name if no abbreviation approved):
- ✅ `BSc in Computer Science`
- ✅ `Diploma in Graphic Design`
- ✅ `AA in Liberal Arts`

### Double/Combination Degrees
Format with ` + `:
- `Bachelor's Degree in Law + Bachelor's Degree in International Relations`
- `BSc in Computer Engineering + BSc in Mathematics`

## 4. URL Rules

- Use **individual programme page URLs** wherever they exist
- URLs must be `=HYPERLINK()` formulas in Excel: `=HYPERLINK("https://...", "https://...")`
- If individual programme pages don't exist, use the **deepest available page**
- If faculty page also doesn't exist, use the general programmes listing page
- **Verify all URLs** return HTTP 200 before including
- A URL that returns 503 from a scraper but loads in a browser = include, flag NMC: Yes
- Never include a URL that silently redirects to the homepage

## 5. Needs Manual Check (NMC) Column

### NMC: Yes — triggers

| Trigger | Reason |
|---|---|
| Korean-medium programme | TOPIK Level 4+ required |
| Japanese-medium programme | JLPT N2+ typically required |
| Russian-medium programme | Russian language proficiency required |
| Chinese-medium programme | HSK Level 4+ required |
| Architecture 5-year programme | Professional degree, verify intake |
| Medicine / Dentistry / Veterinary (included) | Professional degree, complex pathway |
| Branch campus programme | Not confirmed for international students |
| Programme being phased out / new / unconfirmed | Verify still active |
| Hybrid / semipresencial delivery | Verify on-campus component sufficient |
| Diploma pathway leading to Bachelor's | Verify if standalone or articulation only |
| D3 at Indonesian university | Verify open to international students |
| Associate Degree at community college | Verify international student acceptance |
| HND / Foundation Degree (UK) | Verify international eligibility |
| 2-year junior college (Japan/Korea) | Language and eligibility check needed |
| College Diploma (Canada) | Verify international student eligibility |
| Any programme where website shows data entry errors | Flag for manual check |

### NMC: No — default
All English-medium programmes at universities that explicitly welcome international HS grads = NMC: No.

## 6. Excel File Formatting

### Columns (minimum set)
1. **Course Name** — full degree name per naming rules above
2. **Degree Level** — Bachelor / Associate / Diploma
3. **Programme Page URL** — `=HYPERLINK()` formula
4. **Needs Manual Check** — "Yes" or "No"

### Additional columns (add as needed)
- Duration
- Tuition Fee
- Medium of Instruction / Teaching Track
- Intakes / Start Date
- CRICOS Code (Australia)
- School / College / Faculty

### Row Colours
- **Yellow** (`FFF2CC`) = NMC: Yes
- **Green** (`E2EFDA` / `EBF5E1` alternating) = English-medium / NMC: No at non-English universities
- **Blue alternating** (`D6E4F0` / white) = Standard NMC: No rows
- **Orange** (`FCE4D6`) = Diploma and Associate degree rows — to visually separate from bachelor rows
- **Header** = Dark institutional colour (navy, crimson, etc.)

### Other Formatting
- Freeze panes at row 2
- Auto-filter on all columns
- `row_dimensions[i].height = 18` for standard rows
- URL column: blue underlined font (`0563C1`, `underline="single"`)
- NMC: Yes font: amber/brown (`7F6000`)
- English-track font at Korean/Japanese/Russian universities: green (`375623`)
- Diploma/Associate rows font: dark orange (`833C00`)

## 7. Country-Specific Rules

### Spain
- **Exclude:** Medicina (6yr), Odontología (5yr), Farmacia (5yr), Veterinaria (6yr)
- **Include with NMC: Yes:** Fundamentos de Arquitectura (5yr)
- **BAES / BASUS** joint degrees = EXCLUDE
- Double degrees = INCLUDE (separate rows)
- Translate all Spanish names to English
- No standard Diploma/Associate system in Spain

### South Korea
- All Korean-medium programmes → NMC: Yes
- Architecture 5-year → NMC: Yes
- English-medium tracks → NMC: No
- 2-year college diploma (전문학사) → INCLUDE, NMC: Yes if Korean medium
- Duration: Bachelor = 4 years, Architecture = 5 years, 2-year college = 2 years

### Japan
- All Japanese-medium programmes → NMC: Yes
- English-medium programmes → NMC: No
- Tandai / Junior college (短期大学士) → INCLUDE, NMC: Yes if Japanese medium
- Duration: Bachelor = 4 years, Junior college = 2 years

### Indonesia
- Exclude: Employee Class, PJJ (online), Conversion Class, Profession Study Program
- S1 (Bachelor) = 4 years — INCLUDE
- D3 (Diploma III) = 3 years — INCLUDE if open to international HS grads, NMC: Yes
- D4 (Diploma IV) = 4 years — INCLUDE if open to international HS grads, NMC: Yes
- Mark D3/D4 in italic green
- Medicine: Include with NMC: Yes
- Hospitality at branch campus (UPH Surabaya/Medan) → NMC: Yes
- Fees: Year 1 total (Semester 1 + Semester 2)

### Russia
- All Russian-medium programmes → NMC: Yes
- English-medium tracks → NMC: No

### China (Mainland)
- Chinese-medium → NMC: Yes
- English-medium → NMC: No
- Chinese Language & Literature = 3 years (all others 4 years at HIT)

### Canada
- Exclude DEC-BAC programmes
- Exclude post-graduate certificates/diplomas (require prior degree)
- French-medium: include, NMC: Yes
- College Diploma (2-3 years) → INCLUDE if open to international HS grads, NMC: Yes
- Advanced Diploma (3 years) → INCLUDE if open to international HS grads, NMC: Yes

### Australia
- Include CRICOS code column
- Duration in months
- List intakes as month names
- Associate Degree → INCLUDE with CRICOS code
- Diploma → INCLUDE if on campus and min 8 months, with CRICOS code
- Advanced Diploma → INCLUDE with CRICOS code
- TAFE certificates under 8 months → EXCLUDE

### United Kingdom
- Foundation Degree (FdA, FdSc) → INCLUDE, NMC: Yes — verify international eligibility
- Higher National Diploma (HND) → INCLUDE, NMC: Yes — verify international eligibility
- Higher National Certificate (HNC) → EXCLUDE — too short
- All other UK diplomas under 8 months → EXCLUDE

### United States
- Associate of Arts (AA) → INCLUDE
- Associate of Science (AS) → INCLUDE
- Associate of Applied Science (AAS) → INCLUDE
- Typically at community colleges — NMC: Yes (verify international student acceptance)
- Duration: 2 years

### Finland
- Duration in ECTS credits + years
- Fee = €11,000/year for non-EU/EEA; free for EU/EEA
- No standard Diploma/Associate system — Bachelor's only

### Kuwait / UAE / Saudi Arabia
- English-medium → NMC: No
- Confirm programmes open to international HS grads
- Some institutions offer Diploma programmes — INCLUDE if eligible

### Germany
- No standard Diploma anymore (old Diplom = EXCLUDE)
- No Associate Degree system
- Bachelor's only

### Guyana / Caribbean
- Medicine premed pathways open to HS grads = Include, NMC: Yes
- 4-Year MD requiring prior undergraduate credits = Exclude

## 8. Fees Formatting

- Always state **currency and period**
- Bachelor: per year or per semester — whichever the university publishes
- Diploma: per year or total programme fee — whichever the university publishes
- Associate Degree: per year or per semester
- Indonesian D3/D4: Year 1 total (Semester 1 + Semester 2), same rule as S1
- Korean: per semester (KRW)
- Australian: total programme fee (AUD)
- Finnish: per academic year (EUR)
- Japanese: per year (JPY)
- When fee PDFs are linked but not scrapable: put the fee PDF URL in the column

## 9. Translation Rules

- All non-English programme names → translate to English
- Use Proper Title Case in English
- Licenciatura countries → translate to "Bachelor's Degree in X"
- D3/D4 (Indonesia) → "Diploma III in X" / "Diploma IV in X"
- 전문학사 (Korea) → "Associate Degree in X"
- 短期大学士 (Japan) → "Associate Degree in X"
- S1 (Indonesia) → "Bachelor's Degree in X" unless more specific name available
- Abbreviations: translate to full English name unless university uses abbreviation officially
- For double/combination degrees: translate both halves and join with ` + `

---

## 10. Scraping Notes

- **503/403 from scraper ≠ broken URL** — many sites block bots but work in browsers
- Always cross-check against the university's official admission guide PDF if available
- When a site is JS-rendered, check for Next.js `buildId` in the HTML
- For Korean universities: the OIA/admissions guidebook PDF is the most reliable source
- For Indonesian universities: the official programme page (`/program/`) is most reliable
- For diploma programmes: check the college/vocational section separately from degree programmes
- For associate degrees at US community colleges: check the catalogue PDF — often most complete
- Verify individual programme URLs exist (HTTP 200 or confirmed via Google) before including
